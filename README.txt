SQL Project - Queries on World Population Data

Overview of Project:
Work with a dataset of world population by country data from recent years. Using the queries, retrieve data and answer the questions below.

Questions:
1) What years are covered by the dataset?
2) What is the largest population size for Gabon in this dataset?
3) What were the 10 lowest population countries in 2005?
4) What are all the distinct countries with a population of over 100 million in the year 2010?
5) How many countries are in this dataset have the word "Islands" in their name?
6) What is the difference in population between 2000 and 2010 in Indonesia?

Answers Obatained Through Queries:
1) 2000 - 2010
2) Gabon had its largest population in 2010 with 1.54 Million
3) The countries with the lowest population in 2005 were (Ascending Order):
	- Niue
	- Falkland Islands (Islas Malvinas)
	- Montserrat
	- Saint Pierre and Miquelon
	- Saint Helena
	- Nauru
	- Cook Islands
	- Turks and Caicos Islands
	- Virgin Islands, British
	- Gibraltar
4) All the Distinct Countries with populations over 100 million in 2010 were:
	- Mexico
	- United States
	- Brazil
	- Russia
	- Nigeria
	- Bangladesh
	- China
	- India
	- Indonesia
	- Japan
	- Pakistan
5) There were 9 countries with the word 'Islands' in their names
6) The difference between the Indonesian populations in 2010 and 2000 were 28.29 Million
